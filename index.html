E1 - 2049 TS WKL
The 2049 is our first attempt at producing a classic-modern hybrid typing experience. Greg is an avid mechanical keyboard enthusiast and a passionate engineer, his two worlds combine in the 2049 creating a engineered mechanical keyboard masterpiece. He wanted to maintain a traditional look on the 2049, with the first designs not including the touch screen display. The modern features added to this board were included with the intention to not disrupt the feel and astesteic of a classic wkl for the mechanical keyboard veterans of the community, while also adding some new and interesting features to help spark a passion amonst new memebers of the hobby. The 2049 was designed with the intention of bringing a new and exciting custom machanical keyboard to your desk and we hope that you see those intention brought to life when you type on it.

E1 Industries
E1 Industries was founded in 2021 by Greg Janes as a way to share his passion projects with the world. Initially started to market his keyboard designs, E1 quickly transformed into a design company creating products for the future. The art styles of Blade Runner 2049, Cyberpunk 2077, Cowboy Bebop, and more have had great influence on the design and creation of the projects, with the 2049 TKL keyboard naming convention honoring its Blade Runner "Spinner" inspiration. The E1 Industries designs are aimed at creating products that are sustainable, upgradable, and functional. We think that products should be made for and owned by the consumer, without a subscription service required for their use.
